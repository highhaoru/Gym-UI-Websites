Row-Fit

This is a one-hour strength and functional training workout that incorporates short cardio intervals. Equipment used: rower machine, treadmill, elliptical machine, air bike, dumbbell, suspension training, balance ball, kettlebell, resistance band

HIIT and Abs

This is a one-hour total body burn, heart pumping, strength and cardiovascular conditioning workout. The concept of HIIT involves intervals of maximum effort followed by a period of rest. This class combines strength training with cardio bursts and is designed to help stimulate fat burning, building lean muscle and a strong core, and maximizing calorie burn both during and after the workout.

Strength Training Bootcamp

This is a one-hour class designed to help build strength and endurance while shedding excess fat. It includes circuit training, body weight exercises as well as core training for a complete workout. It is an athletic-based sculpting class that will give you that extra push and challenge you every time. This class will work your major muscle groups to define and strengthen your entire body.

Kickboxing

This is a one-hour calorie burning class that combines kickboxing movements, coordination and flexibility improvement, and lean muscle build up. Equipment used: punching bag, treadmill, medicine ball, jump rope, resistance band
